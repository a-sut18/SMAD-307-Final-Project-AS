About Handicap

Being a Scratch Golfer Means a 0.0 Handicap... But what is a handicap?

You don't calculate your handicap until you break 100 for the first time typically, shooting a 100 is a 28 handicap and also where the fun begins.

A golf handicap is a numerical measure of an amateur golfer's potential, allowing players of different skill levels to compete fairly. It represents teh number of strokes a player can deduct from their gross score, with a lower handicap indicating a better player.

It is calculated by averaging the best 8 of a players last 20 scores
